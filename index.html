CRISP-DM METHODOLOGY

The Cross-Industry Standard Process for Data Mining remains the world's most popular framework for data science projects.

Definition & Origin

CRISP-DM is a comprehensive data mining methodology that provides a structured approach to planning a data science project. It was established in 1996 by a consortium of prominent companies including NCR, ISL (Integral Solutions Ltd), and Daimler-Benz.

It was designed to be industry-agnostic, meaning it can be applied to any data mining project regardless of the domain or the tools used.
