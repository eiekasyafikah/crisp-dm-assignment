Methodology Overview

Purpose & Applications

CRISP-DM is used to standardize the data mining process. It ensures that technical activities are always aligned with business goals, making it the preferred choice for large-scale enterprise projects.

1. Business Understanding

Defining project objectives and requirements from a business view.

2. Data Understanding

Initial data collection and quality checking.

3. Data Preparation

Cleaning and formatting data for modeling (takes ~80% of time).

4. Modeling

Applying mathematical and statistical algorithms.

5. Evaluation

Verifying if the results meet the initial business goals.

6. Deployment

Delivering final insights or systems to the end-users.

Iterative Process Diagram

Advantages & Limitations

  • PRO: Flexible and iterative; allows teams to move backwards if data is poor.
  • PRO: Strong focus on business ROI rather than just technology.
  • CON: Can be time-consuming due to the heavy data preparation phase.
  • CON: Might feel linear for modern "Agile" software development.

Industry Adoption
